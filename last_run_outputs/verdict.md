The entire roadmap pack (Steps 0-6) is fundamentally flawed, incoherent, and unrealistic. BLOCKING ISSUES
· **Incredible 12-Month End State (`Step_1`):** `Step_1` must be revised to establish a genuinely credible 12-month end state that reflects realistic biological, bioprocess, and analytical development timelines, emphasizing iterative progress and robust definitions of "locked and repeatable processes."
· **Incomplete & Mis-scored Capabilities (`Step_2`, `Step_3`):** `Step_2` must be reconstructed as a MECE map of all critical capabilities (strain engineering, bioprocess, operational, analytical). `Step_3`'s scoring logic must be revised to accurately reflect strategic value, technical difficulty, and the true importance of product quality and analytical validation.
· **Indefensible Scope Selection (`Step_4`):** All capabilities in `Step_4` must be re-categorized based on the revised `Step_1`, `Step_2`, and `Step_3` outputs, ensuring no critical 'Enabling Foundations' or 'Visible End-State' items are wrongly deferred.
· **Unsound Milestone Backbone & Workstreams (`Step_5`):** Milestones in `Step_5` must be redefined to directly reflect revised 'Visible end-state capabilities' and contribute to a credible `Step_1` outcome. `Step_5`'s shared workstream architecture must comprehensively cover all 'Non-negotiable enabling foundations' (e.g., equipment maintenance, supply chain, data infrastructure).
· **Unenforceable Stage-Gate Logic & Unrealistic Realism (`Step_6`):** `Step_6`'s stage-gate logic must be overhauled to define objective, measurable, and realistic 'Exit criteria' for all gates, incorporating specific metrics for biological performance, process repeatability (e.g., N=3 runs with <15% CV), and Product Quality Attributes (PQAs). Continuous processes like 'Strain stability' must be redefined as iterative checkpoints. `Step_6` must also include realistic dependencies, risks, and lead times (e.g., for bioprocess equipment, facility modifications, biological development), and incorporate critical missing gates like a 'Campaign Readiness Gate' and explicit bioreactor characterization gates.
