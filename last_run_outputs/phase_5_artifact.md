## Implications for Month-by-Month Sequencing
The unanimous verdict is that `Step_6` is fundamentally unrealistic and unworkable. The identified dependencies, risks, and lead times are severely underestimated, and the formal gate register lacks the robustness and actionability required for a wet lab environment. This means that any month-by-month sequencing derived from the original `Step_6` would be based on false premises, leading to inevitable delays, missed targets, and operational chaos. Before proceeding to detailed sequencing, `Step_6` must be thoroughly revised to incorporate realistic biological, operational, and analytical constraints, establish objective and measurable gate criteria, and define comprehensive mitigation strategies that genuinely reflect the challenges of early-stage wet lab development.
