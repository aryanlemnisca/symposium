## Implications for Scope Selection and Roadmap Design
The findings from Phase 2 indicate that the bio capability map (`Step_2`) and scoring logic (`Step_3`) are fundamentally flawed and cannot be used as a reliable basis for `Step_4` (Roadmap Scope Selection). The map is incomplete, not MECE, and the scoring is biased and inconsistent. Proceeding with the original documents would lead to a roadmap that is biologically unachievable, operationally unexecutable, analytically unreliable, and strategically misaligned. Significant revisions to `Step_2` and `Step_3` are required to incorporate missing capabilities, correct scoring biases, and align principles with Lemnisca's early-stage, iterative development needs before `Step_4` can be defensibly constructed.
